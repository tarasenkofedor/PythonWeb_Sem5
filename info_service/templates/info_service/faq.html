FAQ - stands for "Frequently Asked Questions."

What types of devices do you repair?

We repair a wide range of devices, including smartphones, laptops, tablets, and more. Feel free to contact us with specific inquiries.

How long does a typical repair take?

The repair time can vary depending on the type and extent of the damage. We strive to complete most repairs within 24-48 hours.

How much will my repair cost?

The cost of repair depends on the device and the extent of the damage. We provide free estimates, so bring your device in for an assessment.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards, and mobile payment apps for your convenience.

Do you offer warranties on your repairs?

Yes, we provide a limited warranty on our repair services. Please ask for details when you bring in your device.

What if I'm not satisfied with the repair?

We aim for customer satisfaction. If you encounter any issues after a repair, please contact us, and we will address it promptly.
